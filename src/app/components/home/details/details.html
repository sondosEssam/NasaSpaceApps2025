Understanding Air Pollution

Key concepts to know about the air we breathe.

factory

Definition of Air Pollution

Harmful pollutants in the air (dust, smoke, gases) that affect health and the environment.

lung_prescriptions

Affected Organs

Primarily affects the lungs, heart, and brain, but can harm other organs as well.

science

Major Diseases

Can lead to stroke, heart disease, COPD, lung cancer, pneumonia, and cataracts.

gas_meter

Key Pollutants

Includes PM2.5, PM10, Carbon Monoxide (CO), Ozone (O₃), Nitrogen Dioxide (NO₂), and Sulphur Dioxide (SO₂).

groups

High-Risk Groups

Children, the elderly, and pregnant women are particularly vulnerable to the effects of air pollution.

hourglass_empty

Exposure Duration

Short-term exposure can cause infections and asthma attacks, while long-term exposure leads to chronic diseases.
